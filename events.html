Aashadi

Magh is celebrated in Pandharpur primarily through Maghi Ekadashi, a significant pilgrimage where lakhs of devotees visit the Vitthal-Rukmini temple.
The celebration, also known as Maghi Vari, though the crowds are smaller than those two festivals

Kartiki Ekadashi

Kartiki Ekadashi is celebrated in the Vitthala Rukmini Temple in Pandharpur, attracting a massive gathering of devotees for the Pandharpur Yatra.

Chaitra Ekadashis

Chaitra Ekadashi (also known as Kamada Ekadashi) is celebrated at the Pandharpur temple with a pilgrimage called Chaitra Wari,
where devotees walk in processions called Dindis to the temple.
